IN POSITION (IP)
Realizes ~85–100% of its equity
  • Acts last every street
  • Sees opponents act first
  • Controls the pot size
  • Can take free cards
OUT OF POSITION (OOP)
Realizes ~60–85% of its equity
  • Acts first every street
  • Commits chips blind
  • Can’t take free cards
  • Pot runs away
VS
Same cards, opposite results — the seat does it.
